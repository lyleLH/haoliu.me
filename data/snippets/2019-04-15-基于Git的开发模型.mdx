### 关键分支（永久的）

- `master`
- `develop`

### 辅助分支（临时的）

- relase
  - **May** checkout from `develop`
  - **Must** merge into `master` and `develop`
  - deleted when merged, **Then** `tag` on `master`
- feature
  - **May** checkout from `develop`
  - **Must** merge into `develop` or `release`
  - deleted when merged
- hotfixes
  - **May** checkout from `master`
  - **Must** merge back into `develop` and `master`
  - deleted when merged
