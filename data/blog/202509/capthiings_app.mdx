# Privacy Policy for Cap Thiings

**Effective Date:** [Sep 01, 2025]  
**Last Updated:** [Sep 01, 2025]

## Introduction

Cap Things ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Cap Things (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Camera and Photos

- **Camera Access**: We require camera permission to enable object recognition features
- **Photos**: Images you capture are processed locally on your device for object recognition
- **Photo Storage**: You can choose to save photos to your personal learning collection within the app

### 2. Learning Data

- **Vocabulary Progress**: Your learning progress, saved words, and personal collections
- **Usage Analytics**: General app usage patterns to improve our services
- **Device Information**: Basic device type and operating system version

### 3. Account Information (If Applicable)

- **Optional Registration**: Email address if you choose to create an account
- **Preferences**: Your learning preferences and app settings

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses:

- **Object Recognition**: Process photos to identify objects and provide English learning content
- **Personalized Learning**: Track your progress and customize learning experiences
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
- **Content Delivery**: Provide relevant English explanations, articles, and vocabulary

### Data Processing:

- **Local Processing**: Most photo recognition happens directly on your device
- **Secure Transmission**: When cloud processing is needed, data is encrypted and transmitted securely
- **No Photo Storage**: We do not permanently store your photos on our servers unless you explicitly save them to your personal collection

## Information Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

- **Service Providers**: Trusted partners who help us provide AI recognition services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **App Stores**: Basic analytics shared with app store platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play)

## Data Security

### Security Measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Local Storage**: Personal collections and progress are stored securely on your device
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized personnel have access to any server-side data
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update our security measures

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:

- **Access**: Request information about data we have collected
- **Delete**: Remove your account and associated data
- **Correct**: Update or correct your personal information
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection
- **Export**: Download your learning progress and saved collections

### Data Retention:

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Learning Progress**: Stored locally on your device
- **Analytics**: Anonymized usage data retained for up to 2 years

## Children's Privacy

Cap Things is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:

- **AI Recognition Services**: For advanced object identification
- **Analytics Providers**: For app performance monitoring (anonymized data only)
- **Translation Services**: For providing accurate English content

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

- **In-app notifications**
- **Email notifications** (if you have an account)
- **Updated "Last Modified" date** at the top of this policy
